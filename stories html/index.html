Claudio Francesco Bellini

Italy

This Politecnico University graduate's strong fascination with creativity, is rooted in his early childhood years, inspired by his father Mario Bellini, whom counts as one of the most affluent figures in design history.

  • profile
  • latest work

This Politecnico University graduate's strong fascination with creativity, is rooted in his early childhood years, inspired by his father Mario Bellini, whom counts as one of the most affluent figures in design history. He collaborated with father, taking part of many types of projects and competitions in architecture and design worldwide. In 1996 he founded Atelier Bellini, an innovative design studio with special interest and an original approach on the relationship between new technologies and the creative processes. In 1998, his revolutionary new office furniture system, TW Collection (Frezza), introduced a completely new vision in the contemporary office landscape. From that point on, his extensive production of innovative projects in this area, has deeply contributed to anticipating behaviors and trends in the working environment evolution over the last 15 years. Today Claudio Bellini Design, recognized as one of the most influential European studios, is active within several fields of architecture.

Born in Bedong, a small city north of Malaysia. PH Teoh is a System Analyst by profession and graduated in University of Windsor, Canada in 1985. PH Teoh has worked in the family business in his early years in various industries until 1991 he started venture into wood industries operating sawmilling, kiln drying plant and manufacturing of moulded components for the furniture industries. Then, It was in 2003 that he started to produce his own designs. He is motivated by passion for quality, aesthetic value, functionality and innovative design and these are the main ingredients that he strive for on each of his product. From 2005 to 2012, he has earned a few awards and recognition during the furniture exhibition in Malaysia Furniture International Fair which was held every March of the year.
